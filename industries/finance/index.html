1. Identity Document Validation and Data Extraction

Streamline your customer verification process by using AI to classify, validate and extract data from identity documents such as passports, driver’s licenses, and national ID cards in most countries. The AI analyzes document images to authenticate them against known security features and extracts relevant data, ensuring quick and accurate identity verification.

  • Enhanced Security: Leverages advanced AI algorithms to reduce invalid or fraudulent identity documents.
  • Increased Efficiency: Automates data entry processes, reducing manual errors and accelerating customer onboarding.
  • Improved User Experience: Offers a seamless verification process for users, enhancing customer satisfaction.

2. Customer support chatbot using LLMs

An AI customer support chatbot, leveraging a Large Language Model (LLM), offers personalized financial advice and assistance to both new and existing customers. The integration of Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) enhances the chatbot’s precision by aligning responses more closely with factual content specific to the bank’s offerings and the customer’s individual circumstances. This combination ensures both contextually relevant and accurate support.

  • 24/7 Support: Human-driven support agents are typically not available at all hours of the day. The AI assistant can manage customer queries at scale 24 hours a day.
  • Reduced Costs: An AI-driven assistant can reduce the cost of delivering ongoing customer support dramatically whilst being able to serve multiple customers concurrently.
  • Personalized Interactions: The AI chatbot can utilize a customer’s banking profile and historical interactions with the bank to ensure that the queries are personalized and relevant to the customer.
